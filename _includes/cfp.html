Call for presenters

We are calling for presenters for Oslo BSides Digital Edition, and our CFP is open until April 30th 2021.

All the presentations will be prerecorded, and the recording will be broadcasted during the virtual conference. After each presentation, there will be a live Q&A hosted by a moderator. The presentations need to be between 20 and 25 minutes, and we will schedule an additional 10 minutes for the Q&A.

We love to see brand new speakers, seasoned speakers, and everyone in between! Topics of interest include (but are in no way limited to) the following, preference given to talks that actually provide solutions as well as insight to problems:

  • Information technology
  • Network security
  • Web Application security
  • Mobile security
  • Virtualization and cloud computing
  • Innovative attack / defense strategies
  • Forensics / Malware
  • Embedded device security / Internet of things
  • Biometrics
  • Hardware hacking
  • Phone phreaking
  • Biohacking
  • Open source software
  • Evolutionary computing
  • Robotics (bonus points for bringing an actual robot)
  • Massive abuse of technology

You can submit your proposal using our submission form until April 30th 2021. For any questions regarding the CFP, contact {{ site.emails.cfp }}.
